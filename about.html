Our group worked on the Silent Auction website, which allows for running auctions that can be bid on from anywhere in the world, completely over the internet. The group is made up of one unit 1 student, 2 unit 2s, 2 unit 3s, and 2 unit 4s. Below you will find more info about each of us, including relevant links.
